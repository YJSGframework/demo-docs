Use any number of accordions shortcodes within your Joomla articles or modules.
They can easily be added inside the editor by using [yjsgfa name="fa fa-picture-o"] Accordions shortcode form located under text editor or by adding shortcodes directly.

examples:


[yjsgacs id="myid"]
