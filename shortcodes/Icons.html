Use any number of icons shortcodes within your Joomla articles or modules.
They can easily be added inside the editor by using [yjsgfa name="fa fa-picture-o"] Icons shortcode form located under text editor or by adding shortcodes directly.

example:

[yjsgfa name="fa fa-music"]    [yjsgfa name="fa fa-heart fa-border"]    [yjsgfa name="fa fa-flag fa-2x"]    [yjsgfa name="fa fa-clock-o fa-3x"]    [yjsgfa name="fa fa-star fa-4x"]    [yjsgfa name="fa fa-spinner fa-spin fa-5x"]

shortcode:

[yjsgpre pretty="1" scroll="0"] {yjsgfa name="fa fa-music"} {yjsgfa name="fa fa-heart fa-border"} {yjsgfa name="fa fa-flag fa-2x"} {yjsgfa name="fa fa-clock-o fa-3x"} {yjsgfa name="fa fa-star fa-4x"} {yjsgfa name="fa fa-spinner fa-spin fa-5x"} [/yjsgpre]
