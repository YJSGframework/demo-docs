SmoothScroll is jQuery function that auto targets all the anchors in a page and display a smooth scrolling effect upon clicking them.
For a simple use we have created smoothscroll class yjscroll that can be used on your anchors.

options:

Options Description
data-before Stop scroll nth px before the element.
data-after Stop scroll nth px after the element.

example:

  • Scroll to Section 1
  • Scroll to Section 2
  • Scroll to Section 3
  • Scroll to Section 4

markup:

[yjsgpre pretty="1" scroll="0"] Scroll to 1 Scroll to 2 Scroll to 3
